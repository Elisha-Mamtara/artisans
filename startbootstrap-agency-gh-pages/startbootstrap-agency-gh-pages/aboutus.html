Birthed in November, 2019 from the minds of four innovative Social Entrepreneurs who are technologically inclined, EasyConnect is an online business directory serving as a collaborative business partner to artisans and small and medium scale enterprises (“SMEs”) in Nigeria. The portal provides an avenue for small businesses to increase their market coverage by opening up new markets segments for their services via the internet..

To connect our client with the best Artisans at the lowest possible price, and at their utmost convenience while opening up the potential market for artisans to thrive and advertise their services to a much wider audience.

To become the leading digital Artisan firm in Nigeria by building the best linkages with Artisans and providing professional Artisan services to inspire and implement solutions.

Accessibility, Convenient, Dependability, Safety, Collaborative and Professionalism
